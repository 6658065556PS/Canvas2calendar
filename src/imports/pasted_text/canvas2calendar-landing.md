Product Context

Canvas2Calendar helps Berkeley SCET students turn Canvas coursework into organized weekly tasks automatically.

Many SCET students take multiple courses at the same time. Assignments, readings, and deadlines are scattered across Canvas modules, assignments, and syllabi.

Canvas2Calendar solves this by:

• Importing coursework from Canvas
• Converting assignments into structured tasks
• Organizing them into a clear weekly execution plan
• Syncing tasks to Google Calendar

The product is not a generic planner.
It is an automation layer that organizes Canvas coursework.
